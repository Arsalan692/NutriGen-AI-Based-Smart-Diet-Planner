# NutriGen UI Redesign - Fresh & Energetic Theme 🌱

## Overview
Transformed the UI from a dark, gloomy "ghost house" theme to a vibrant, fresh, and energetic nutrition-focused design that makes users feel motivated and healthy!

---

## 🎨 Color Palette Transformation

### Before (Dark Theme)
- Background: `#0d1a0d` (Very dark green - gloomy)
- Cards: `#132213` (Dark forest green)
- Text: `#edf5e5` (Light on dark)
- Accent: `#6fcf3e` (Neon green)

### After (Fresh Theme)
- Background: `#f8fdf4` (Fresh cream white)
- Cards: `#ffffff` (Pure white with subtle shadows)
- Text: `#2e3b2e` (Dark forest green - readable)
- Accent: `#4caf50` (Vibrant healthy green)

**Impact:** The new palette evokes freshness, health, and energy - perfect for a nutrition app!

---
